Return and Replacement Policy

Return Policy

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery if the product has any manufacturing defect or is not as described. The product must be in its original condition with all accessories, packaging, and documentation.

Non-Returnable Conditions

No return on damaged and misuse of product: Products that show physical damage, liquid damage, or signs of misuse will not be eligible for return. Software issues caused by customer-installed programs or OS modifications are also not covered.

Replacement Policy

We offer a 15-day replacement policy for defective products. If the laptop develops a hardware issue within 15 days of purchase, we will replace it with the same or equivalent model subject to availability and verification of the defect by our technicians.

Warranty Information

All laptops come with a 6-month warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal usage conditions. The warranty does not cover physical damage, liquid damage, or issues caused by unauthorized repairs or modifications.
